Dark mode is now available to all ruqqies (ruqquteers or ruqqusians??). You can still unlock sweet profile badges by referring your friends.

We understand not everyone wants to see all 1A-protected speech such as racial slurs. You can now hide comments and posts including words found here. This setting is user-controlled and toggled off by default.

To make posting easier, you can now paste links into Ruqqus. You'll be redirected to the submission page with the link and title fields automagically filled in!
