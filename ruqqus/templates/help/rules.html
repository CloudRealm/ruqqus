Ruqqus Content Policy

Ruqqus is a free speech environment. However, in order to comply with US law, and in order to ensure the safety of our users, there are a few things you are not permitted to do with the Ruqqus platform.

By using Ruqqus, you agree to follow these rules. Violations (in Ruqqus's sole discretion) may lead to the suspension of your Ruqqus account.

1. Intellectual Property

You may not upload to, embed within, or link out from Ruqqus:

  • Copyrighted material that you are not authorized to distribute
  • Anything not legal to publish within, or export from, the United States of America

2. Digital Safety

You may not upload to, embed within, or link out from Ruqqus:

  • Digitally malicious content, including but not limited to:
    • Phishing attacks
    • XSS attacks
  • URL shorteners
  • Anything that breaks Ruqqus
  • If you find a problem, tell us about it. You'll get a nifty badge on your profile.

3: User Safety

You may not use Ruqqus to do any of the following:

  • Threaten or intimidate other users
  • Impersonate Ruqqus staff.
  • Solicit, collect, or publish personal information, either yours or another user's
  • Sexual or sexually suggestive material not marked "NSFW"
  • Sexual or sexually suggestive material involving individuals under the age of 18
  • Spam

4. IRL Safety

You may not use Ruqqus to do any of the following

  • Incite, plan, or execute unlawful or violent activity
  • This includes sale of illicit substances.
  • This does not include the exercise of the human right to free speech, where the speech is unlawful in a user's home country but protected in the United States
